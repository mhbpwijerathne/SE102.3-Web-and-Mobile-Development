Why Trees Are Important

Planting trees save our planet by combating the effects of greenhouse gas

Planting trees save our planet by combating the effects of greenhouse gas. According to research, an adult tree can store 13 pounds of carbon or change 48 pounds into oxygen all in one year. An acre full of trees stores 2.6 tons of carbon dioxide in one year.
